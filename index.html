About

I'm an Electrical Engineer with a Master's and a Ph.D. in Engineering and over a decade of experience in data analytics and AI.

I currently work at Microsoft, specializing in Generative AI, Large Language Models, and copilots within the M365 ecosystem — following a tenure at Microsoft Research. My work blends research and engineering, from evaluating AI models to shipping impactful real-world solutions, leading to multiple patents and publications.

I've mentored 300+ students and consulted for companies such as Shell, Fastshop, Symrise, and Petrobras on advanced AI and ML initiatives.
